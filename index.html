About us

We are high schoolers who like to code, of all varying skill levels. We practice coding and prepare for competitions, like the Advent of Code and the Canadian Computing Competition, run by the University of Waterloo.
If this sounds intimidating, don't worry! The competitions are optional and we like to have fun at our meetings.

FAQ

How do I join?

Join our Discord, follow our Instagram, and come to our meetings in room 231 on Thursdays at lunch!

I don't know how to code, can I still join?

Absolutely! Everyone is welcome, and you'll meet people who can help you and you'll learn new things.

When and where?

Room 231 on Thursdays at lunch. Check our Instagram to see if a meeting is cancelled.
